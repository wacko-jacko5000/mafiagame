# Seasons Rules

- A season starts in `draft`.
- Only one season may be `active` at a time.
- Activating a season automatically deactivates any other active season.
- Deactivation is explicit and only applies to an active season.
- `startsAt` and `endsAt` are informational in this phase. They do not trigger automatic activation or deactivation.
- Season records are structural only in this phase. No gameplay rewards, resets, leaderboard splits, or event schedules are inferred from them yet.
