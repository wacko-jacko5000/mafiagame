# Market Rules

- Trades must settle atomically across market listings, inventory ownership, and player cash.
- Only the owner of an inventory item may list it.
- Equipped items cannot be listed.
- Listed items are locked and cannot be equipped or listed again while the listing is active.
- Only the seller may cancel an active listing.
- Buyers must have enough cash before settlement succeeds.
- Buying a listing transfers the inventory item to the buyer, transfers cash from buyer to seller, and marks the listing sold.
- Auctions, bids, gang markets, escrow, reputation, fees, taxes, and dynamic pricing are intentionally out of scope.
